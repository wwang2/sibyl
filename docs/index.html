How It Works

Sibyl operates through a sophisticated multi-agent system that continuously monitors information sources, processes evidence, and generates predictions. The system is designed to be both powerful and transparent, ensuring that every prediction can be traced back to its source material.

The Prediction Process

  1. Signal Discovery: Automated agents scan RSS feeds, news sources, and other data streams for emerging patterns
  2. Evidence Collection: Relevant information is gathered and stored with complete provenance tracking
  3. AI Assessment: Advanced language models analyze the evidence and generate probabilistic predictions
  4. Validation & Learning: Predictions are tracked and the system learns from outcomes to improve accuracy

Trust & Transparency

In an era where AI predictions can seem like black boxes, Sibyl prioritizes transparency and accountability. Every prediction includes detailed reasoning, source citations, and confidence levels, allowing users to understand not just what will happen, but why the system believes it will happen.

🔍 Full Provenance Tracking

Every prediction is fully traceable back to its source evidence, with timestamps, confidence scores, and detailed reasoning provided for complete transparency.
